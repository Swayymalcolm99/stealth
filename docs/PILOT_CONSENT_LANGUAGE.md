# Concierge Pilot: Informed Consent Language

**Effective Date:** June 2026  
**Pilot Duration:** Two weeks (14 days from enrollment)

---

## Introduction

Thank you for your interest in the Stealth Concierge Pilot. This document explains what participation involves, how your data will be handled, and your rights as a pilot participant.

**Please read this carefully before agreeing to participate.**

---

## What Is This Pilot?

Stealth is building a private, programmable email system where you decide who can reach your inbox. This pilot tests whether restrictive access policies—allow, request approval, block, or require payment—actually work for real people in real workflows.

We're enrolling 8-12 users for two weeks to answer one core question:

> **Will users keep restrictive policies enabled and report lower email triage effort?**

Your participation helps us validate the core promise before building expensive infrastructure.

---

## What Will You Do?

### Required Activities
1. **Configure policies** - You'll set up rules for allow (trusted), request (approval), block, and paid-access senders
2. **Use your inbox normally** - Send and receive mail with your configured policies in effect
3. **Report issues** - Flag any senders incorrectly blocked or delayed (false positives)
4. **Use the dashboard** - View your policy metrics and provide optional feedback

### Time Commitment
- **Setup:** 15-20 minutes (policy configuration)
- **Ongoing:** No active time required; measurement is automatic
- **Feedback:** Optional 30-minute check-in call (recommended)
- **Total:** ~1 hour over two weeks

### No Special Requirements
You don't need to:
- Change how you use email
- Install additional software (uses standard Stealth client)
- Pay anything or use real postage (pilot payments are subsidized)
- Keep your policy enabled the entire time (we measure retention, not forced compliance)

## What Data Will We Collect?

### Data We Collect
- **Policy decisions:** Which rule was applied to each sender
- **Delivery outcomes:** Whether the message reached your inbox
- **Override frequency:** How often you manually changed an automatic decision
- **False positive reports:** Which senders you reported as incorrectly blocked
- **Policy configuration:** Your rules and their timestamps

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- **Message bodies** - Never stored, never accessible to pilots or team
- **Sender email addresses** - Hashed once, then permanently discarded
- **Message subjects** - Never stored
- **Your identity** - Identified only by pilot ID, not by name or email
- **Any metadata** that could expose communication patterns

### Privacy Method: Hashing
Sender identifiers are converted to one-way hashes (mathematical fingerprints). We can count "Alice sent you 5 messages" without knowing "alice@example.com" or what she sent.

## How Long Will Data Be Kept?

| Data Type | Retention Period | Your Control |
|-----------|------------------|--------------|
| Policy decisions | 2 weeks after pilot ends | Export anytime; auto-delete after 2 weeks unless you request archival |
| Override records | 2 weeks after pilot ends | Export anytime; auto-delete after 2 weeks unless you request archival |
| Policy configuration | 2 weeks after pilot ends | Export anytime; keep indefinitely if archived |
| Personal identifiers | Pilot duration only | Deleted day 1 after pilot ends (no exception) |
| Cohort analysis (aggregated) | Indefinite | Published anonymously after pilot completion |

All data is deleted or archived per your instructions by Day 28 post-pilot.

## What Will We Do With Your Data?

### During the Pilot
- Count policy decisions to measure effectiveness
- Flag trends (e.g., many overrides of one rule) for concierge support
- Generate your personal metrics dashboard

### After the Pilot
- Calculate per-user success metrics (retention, triage reduction)
- Analyze cohort patterns to identify requirements updates
- Publish aggregated, anonymized results (no personal data)

### We Will NOT
- Sell your data
- Share individual data with third parties
- Use your data for marketing
- Re-identify you from hashed identifiers
- Train AI models on your data

## Your Participation Consent

By enrolling in this pilot, you agree to:

### 1. Participation Consent
- Your inbox access policies will be tested for effectiveness
- Your policy decisions and manual overrides will be measured
- You're helping validate inbox control design before infrastructure investment

### 2. Data Handling Consent
- Policy measurements and false positive reports will be retained for analysis
- Your data will be hashed to protect privacy
- Individual data will not be shared outside the core pilot team

### 3. Privacy-Safe Analytics Consent
- Measurements use privacy budgets to prevent inference attacks
- Message bodies and personal identifiers will never be stored
- You can export your data or request deletion at any time

### 4. Risk Acknowledgment
- This is an early-stage pilot; bugs or policy failures may occur
- Misconfigurations could block wanted senders or allow unwanted ones
- Stealth will provide support but cannot guarantee perfect accuracy
- You can exit the pilot at any time without penalty

## If Something Goes Wrong

### What If I'm Blocked From Important Mail?
- Report false positives in-app (Metrics → Report Issue)
- Email pilot@stealth.email for urgent support
- We can temporarily adjust your policy while we investigate

### What If I Change My Mind?
- Exit the pilot anytime (Dashboard → Exit Pilot)
- Request data deletion (Metrics → Delete My Data)
- Your data will be erased within 48 hours

### What If There's a Bug?
- Report it in-app or email pilot@stealth.email
- You'll receive support from our concierge team
- Bugs don't count against you or affect metrics
